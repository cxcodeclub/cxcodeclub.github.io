Charing Cross Library

What we do

We're a free computer coding club for children aged 9-11 at Charing Cross Library in the heart of London's West-End. These are the programming languages we teach:

  1. Scratch
  2. Website markup (HTML & CSS)
  3. Python

Here are more advanced projects for you to try:

  • Websites (HTML, CSS & JavaScript)
  • Advanced Websites (HTML, CSS, JavaScript & modern libraries)
  • Ruby

What's next?

Once you're comfortable with HTML and Python try practising your skills at these Saturday clubs:

  • Wizzie Wizzie Computer Coding Club (weekly for 8-14 year olds at Caledonian Rd)
  • Dragon Hall CoderDojo (monthly for 7-17 year olds in Covent Garden)
  • Imperial CoderDojo (monthly for 8-12 year olds at South Kensington)
  • Young Rewired State (monthly for U18s near Houses of Parliament. Organises the free, week-long, national Festival of Code hackathon the first week of the summer holidays.)
